About the Event

The greatest 2 minutes in sports meets Orlando's finest.

Derby Daze Orlando is Central Florida's premier Kentucky Derby-themed day party experience. Watch the race live, dress to impress, and celebrate with Orlando's social community in an atmosphere that blends Southern elegance with modern nightlife energy.

Dress
the Part.

Derby Daze is a fashion moment as much as a party. We expect guests to arrive dressed in the spirit of the Kentucky Derby — elegant, expressive, and celebration-ready. The dress code is strictly enforced at the door.

Women
Bold & bright colors, floral patterns
Dresses, jumpsuits, pantsuits
Rompers, dress shorts
Fascinators, elegant hats, veils
Elevated footwear & accessories
Men
Spring color palette — think pastels & earth tones
Suits, blazers, sport coats
Vests, sweaters, button-downs
Chinos, slacks, dress shorts
Clean dress shoes or loafers
!
Strictly Enforced Absolutely no denim, sports attire, t-shirts, or tank tops will be permitted. Attendees who do not comply with the dress code may be denied entry — no refunds will be issued for dress code violations.
